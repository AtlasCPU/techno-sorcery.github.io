Techno-Sorcery

"Man is still the most extraordinary computer of all."


I've used a lot of computers over the years. This page details the most memorable ones.

My computers

"Lightbox" - My current build:

Years: 2018 - Present
CPU: Intel i7 8700k
GPU: GTX 1060
RAM: Dual-Channel, 16gb 3200mhz DDR4
SSD: 500gb Samsung NVME
HDD: 1TB WD Black, 1TB WD Blue
Case: NZXT S340 Elite Mid-Tower ATX
Notes: PSU and GPU were recycled from Voyager 2.0. I got to build this as a present for graduating middle school. It was a beast then, and is still a beast now.


"Voyager 2.0":

Years: 2016 - 2018
CPU: Intel i5 4690
GPU: GTX 750ti/1060
RAM: Dual-Channel, 16gb 1600mhz DDR3
SSD: 120gb PNY, 240gb PNY, 80gb Intel
HDD: 1TB WD Blue
Case: Corsair 300R Mid-Tower ATX
Notes: This wasn't exactly a distinct build per se, but rather a "Computer of Theseus". As I gradually replaced the lesser parts from the original build with better ones, it reached a point where it was practically a whole new computer. When I upgraded, I gave it to my dad.


"Voyager":

Years: 2015 - 2016
CPU: Intel i5 4690
GPU: GTX 750ti
RAM: Single-Channel, 8gb 1600mhz DDR3
SSD: None
HDD: 1TB WD Blue
Case: Old Dell Dimension Mid-Tower mATX
Notes: I bought most of the parts over the latter half of 2015. I didn't have any money for a GPU, so I just used integrated graphics for the first couple months of owning it. I was obsessed with the Voyager probes for some reason, so I painted the case black and white, and taped on a golden CD (the golden record).


13" Unibody Macbook Pro:

Years: 2012 - Present
CPU: Intel i5 3210M
GPU: Intel HD 4000
RAM: Dual-Channel, 16gb 1600mhz DDR3L
SSD: 240gb Sandisk
HDD: None
Case: 13" Unibody Chassis
Notes: This thing was my first personal computer; I got it for my 9th birthday. It's still my main laptop, and has proven to be one of the most dependable pieces of technology I've ever owned. It ran Windows for a while, but I've since upgraded it to macOS Big Sur (which is apparently "unsupported"- yet it runs flawlessly with OCLP :-) )


2011 Family PC build:

2006 Family PC build:
