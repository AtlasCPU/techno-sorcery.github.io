The OFFICIAL Nerd Test

This is a reverse "purity test", meaning that the more boxes you tick off, the more ______ you are. If you, for example, didn't tick off any boxes, then you'd be 0% ______. This isn't a knowledge quiz, so you can't lose points by ticking off any "wrong" answers-- there are none such things.

Now enjoy the quiz and, if you like it, be sure to share it with your friends.

Possible Outcomes

0% - 9% = Pure
Whelp, you're not a nerd. Case closed. You're a normal person who likes
normal things, and that's cool.

10% - 19% = Well-cultured
You have some nerdy tendencies, but you're still pretty normal. However, your
score could easily swing into 'nerd territory.

20% - 29% = Low-key nerd
You were definitely into many intellectual pursuits, but that never seemed
to impact your social life. You spent good time with both the nerds and the
'cooler' kids, and were well-known around the school.

30% - 39% = Honors student
You were the kid who saw nothing lower than an 'A' and who always made the
honor roll. When others were panicking and trying to raise their D to a C, you
had nothing to worry about.

40% - 49% = Sci-Fi Addict
Star Trek > Sleep... or at least that's how it is in your mind. You've read so
many books and seen so many movies that Douglas Adams, Isaac Asimov, and Kubrick
practically live with you.

50% - 59% = Experienced convention-goer
You're everywhere. All the guys down at the Hamfest know you on a first name
basis, and you've lost count of how many ComicCons you've attended.

60% - 69% = Dungeon Master
Roll a d20 for nerdiness... you rolled a nat 20! That's right, you're the
dungeon master of your guild. After all, what's cooler than a binder covered in D&D stickers?

70% - 79% = Poindexter
When you watched 'Revenge of the Nerds', you couldn't help but relate.
Hell, you're practically every stereotypical nerd combined.

80% - 89% = Human Microchip
You have transcended beyond the mortal plane of existence. Other nerds are
nothing compared you. Your complete devotion to academics and your mannerisms are
enough to turn almost anyone away from you.

90% - 100% = Nerd God
FEAR MY POWER, MERE MORTALS! I SHALL RISE UP AND DESTROY YOU WITH MY REVERSE
POLISH NOTATION CALCULATOR!
