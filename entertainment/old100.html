Ever been told you're an "old soul"? Have an inexplicable affinity for fruitcakes and hard candies? You just might be an undercover old person-- even if you don't look the part!

Old Person Test

This is a reverse "purity test", meaning that the more boxes you tick off, the more ______ you are. If you, for example, didn't tick off any boxes, then you'd be 0% ______. This isn't a knowledge quiz, so you can't lose points by ticking off any "wrong" answers-- there are none such things.

Now enjoy the quiz and, if you like it, be sure to share it with your friends.

Possible Outcomes

0% - 9% = Teenage Hooligan
As far as the elders are concerned, you're a teenage hooligan whose up to no good. You better keep off that lawn of mine, sonny boy...

10% - 19% = Grandma's Favorite
You are certainly grandma's favorite grandchild, and there's no contesting that. Whenever she needs a little help mowing her lawn or baking some cookies, you're right at her side.

20% - 29% = Hard candy enthusiast
Hanging out with grandma every weekend really got you hooked on her favorite sweets. Everyone dreads playing Secret Santa with you, as at least one of them is guaranteed to get stuck with an inedible fruitcake or some hard candies.

30% - 39% = Early Bird Special
There's nothing like waking up at 4AM and groggily driving down to the nearest Denny's for some grub, even if nobody under the age of 60 is up that early.

40% - 49% = Personified hearing aid
Pardon me, what did you say again? Sorry dear, you'll need to come closer. What? You want to dump me in a nursing home?

50% - 59% = Neighborhood Grump
Everybody in the neighborhood knows that you're always watching your yard, waiting to pounce on any kid who dares to step on your lawn or vault a baseball over the fence.

60% - 69% = Never-ending storyteller
Back when I was just a young-un, my grandpappy used to take me down to the old McDonalds or, as we liked to call it in those days, "Mickey Dees". Now, back in those days, you could buy a burger for a nickle. And you could go up to the nice man at the- *zzzzzzzzzzz*

70% - 79% = Personified hearing aid
Pardon me, what did you say again? Sorry dear, you'll need to come closer. What? You want to dump me in a nursing home?

80% - 89% = Most popular person at the senior home
You are, by far, the most popular person down at the senior home. Bingo? Pfffffft, you're nothing less than a hotshot.

90% - 100% = Immortal Being
You've been around for so long that no other old person dares to question your oldness.
